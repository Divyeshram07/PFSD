Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

1. Overview

We understand that circumstances may arise where you need to cancel your order. This cancellation policy outlines the terms under which you may cancel an order and any applicable charges or refunds.

2. Cancellation Window

  • Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placement without incurring any fees.
  • After 24 hours, cancellations may be subject to a restocking or processing fee.

3. Cancellation Process

To cancel an order, please follow these steps:

  • Log in to your account and navigate to the "My Orders" section.
  • Select the order you wish to cancel and click "Cancel Order."
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the cancellation is processed.

4. Refund Policy

Refunds for canceled orders will be processed as follows:

  • For orders canceled within the 24-hour window, a full refund will be issued.
  • For orders canceled after 24 hours, refunds will be issued minus any applicable fees.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

5. Exceptions

Certain items may not be eligible for cancellation or refund, including:

  • Custom-made or personalized items.
  • Perishable goods or items with limited shelf life.
